Tip #1:

Join hackathons. Lots of them.

  • Provide hands-on experience in applying theoretical knowledge to solve real-world problems under time constraints and intense pressure
  • Winning (or even just participating in) a hackathon adds credibility to your resume
  • Projects built can become tangible portfolio pieces that demonstrate your capabilities to employers
  • Gain exposure to different emerging technologies and problem-solving ideas

Tip #1:

Join hackathons. Lots of them.

  • Provide networking opportunities and direct interaction with industry professionals who often serve as mentors or judges
